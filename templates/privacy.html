Privacy Policy

Deadline

What we process

  • Uploaded PDFs are processed in memory to extract their text and are never written to disk or stored.
  • Extracted syllabus text is sent to Google's Gemini API to identify deadlines, subject to Google's API terms. We do not store it.
  • Deadline items you review exist only in your browser until you export them; we do not store them server-side.

Google Calendar access

  • If you connect Google Calendar, we request the calendar.events scope, which is permission to create events only. We use it for one thing: creating the events you explicitly ask us to add.
  • We never read, list, or delete your existing events.
  • OAuth tokens are stored server-side, keyed to your browser session, and are deleted when you click disconnect (which also revokes our access at Google) or automatically after 90 days of inactivity.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no ads, no selling of data.
